The fillable system

A pocket molded right into the bake.

Each PocketPans set includes a silicone insert that nests into the cavity during baking, creating a perfect well in every cupcake, muffin, mini pizza, or pie.

No more coring out cupcakes with a knife. No more hollowing out dough by hand. The pocket is already there — ready for cream, custard, sauce, cheese, ganache, or whatever you imagine.
