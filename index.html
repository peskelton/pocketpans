The fillable system

A pocket molded right into the bake.

Each PocketPans set includes a unique silicone insert that nests into the cavity during baking, creating a perfect well in every cupcake, muffin, mini pizza, or pie that comes out.

No more coring out cupcakes with a paring knife. No more hollowing out pizza dough by hand. The pocket is already there — ready for cream, custard, sauce, cheese, fruit, ganache, or whatever you can imagine.
